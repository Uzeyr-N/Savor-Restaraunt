about us

discover our story

The restaurant was founded by a group of childhood friends who shared a love of food and drink. They grew up in a small town where culinary experiences were limited, but they were all passionate about exploring new flavors and cuisines. As they got older and pursued different careers, they never lost their love of food and wine.

They chose the name "Savoré" because it reflects their philosophy of savoring every moment and every flavor in life.

The owners and chefs are passionate about sharing their knowledge and expertise with others, so they often host wine tastings, cooking classes, and other events to help diners deepen their understanding of the culinary arts.
